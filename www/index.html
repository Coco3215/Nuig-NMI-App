National Museum of Ireland - Archaeology
Kildare Street, Dublin 2

The national repository for all archaeological objects found in Ireland and home to over two million artefacts

Opening Hours:
Tuesday - Saturday: 10am - 5pm
Sunday: 2pm - 5pm
Closed Mondays, Christmas Day and Good Friday

National Museum of Country Life

National Museum of Ireland - Country Life
Turlough Park, Castlebar, Co. Mayo

Home to the national collection of objects representing the traditional way of life in Ireland since 1850.

Opening Hours
Tuesday - Saturday: 10am - 5pm
Sunday: 2pm - 5pm
Closed Mondays, Christmas Day and Good Friday

Museum of Decorative Arts & History

National Museum of Ireland - Decorative Arts & History
Collins Barracks, Benburb Street, Dublin 7

Home to a wide range of objects, which include weaponry, furniture, silver, ceramics and glassware; as well as examples of Folklife and costume.

Opening Hours:
Tuesday - Saturday: 10am - 5pm

National Museum of Natural History

National Museum of Ireland - Natural History
Merrion Street, Dublin 2

Galleries of animals from Ireland and overseas, also geological exhibits from a total collection of about 2 million scientific specimens.

Opening Hours
Tuesday - Saturday: 10am - 5pm
Sunday: 2pm - 5pm
Closed Mondays, Christmas Day and Good Friday
